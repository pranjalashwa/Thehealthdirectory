Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

We begin with a comprehensive intake to understand your current routines, goals, and challenges. From there, we develop a personalized roadmap focusing on nutrition, sleep, stress management, and movement. Ongoing support includes regular check-ins and adjustments to ensure consistency and progress.
Our services are designed for individuals seeking to build sustainable wellness habits and reduce long-term health risks. This is ideal for professionals, busy parents, and anyone looking to optimize their baseline health. We focus on prevention and routine-building, not medical treatment.
We offer both online consultations via secure video conferencing and in-person sessions by appointment. Online sessions provide flexibility and convenience, while in-person meetings are available for those who prefer face-to-face interaction.
We offer three tiers: Foundation (single consultation + plan), Signature (3-month program with regular check-ins), and Concierge (6-month premium program with priority access). Detailed pricing is available on the Consulting page. All packages include personalized plans and ongoing support.
Our Personalization Studio guides you through selecting your wellness goals, dietary preferences, allergen restrictions, and delivery preferences. Based on your inputs, we suggest product options tailored to your needs. These are wellness support products, not medications, and should be discussed with a qualified healthcare professional.
We take privacy seriously. All personal health information is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. Consultations are confidential, and all data handling complies with applicable privacy regulations. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Meet the Owner

Shivi Tyagi

Shivi Tyagi is a Research Scholar at Banasthali Vidyapith, specializing in clinical and therapeutic nutrition with expertise in Food Technology and Processing. She has over six years of experience in clinical nutrition, integrating evidence-based dietary interventions with applied nutritional science. Her research focuses on functional food development, bioactive compounds, and product stability, with an emphasis on improving nutritional quality and health outcomes. She has authored more than five Scopus-indexed research and review papers and contributed to five or more book chapters in the field of food science and nutrition. She is also an innovator, holding patents and copyrights related to food and nutrition technologies, and continues to work toward translating scientific research into practical, health-oriented food solutions.

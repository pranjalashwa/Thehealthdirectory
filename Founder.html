Shivi Tyagi, Founder - The Health Directory

Shivi Tyagi is a Research Scholar at Banasthali Vidyapith, specializing in clinical and therapeutic nutrition with expertise in Food Technology and Processing. She has over six years of experience in clinical nutrition, integrating evidence-based dietary interventions with applied nutritional science. Her research focuses on functional food development, bioactive compounds, and product stability, with an emphasis on improving nutritional quality and health outcomes. She has authored more than five Scopus-indexed research and review papers and contributed to five or more book chapters in the field of food science and nutrition. She is also an innovator, holding patents and copyrights related to food and nutrition technologies, and continues to work toward translating scientific research into practical, health-oriented food solutions.
